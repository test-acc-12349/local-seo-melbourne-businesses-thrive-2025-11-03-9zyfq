5. Cookie Policy

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small files that are stored on your device when you visit our website. They help us remember your preferences, enhance your user experience, and analyze how you use our site.

Types of Cookies We Use

  • Essential Cookies: These cookies are necessary for the website to function properly and cannot be disabled. They enable basic functions like page navigation and access to secure areas.
  • Analytics Cookies: These cookies help us understand how visitors use our website by collecting data about pages visited, time spent, and user interactions. This information helps us improve our services.
  • Marketing Cookies: These cookies track your activity across websites to deliver personalized advertisements and measure the effectiveness of our marketing campaigns.
  • Preference Cookies: These cookies remember your preferences and settings to provide a more personalized experience on future visits.

Managing Cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to refuse cookies or alert you when cookies are being sent. However, blocking or deleting cookies may affect your ability to use certain features of our website. For more information about managing cookies, visit www.allaboutcookies.org.
